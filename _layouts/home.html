The Digital Security Exchange helps the social sector stay safe from digital attack.

The DSX pairs social sector organizations with credible and trustworthy digital security experts and trainers who can help them keep their data and networks safe from exposure, exploitation, and attack.

This project is currently under development, as is this website. However, we are currently working with a limited number of organizations and providers, so if you need digital security assistance please be in touch via one of the methods below or read about our Organizational Partners program here.
